# Published Posts Source Guide

`content/posts/` 是对外发布层，不直接等同于 wiki 本体。

## Current role

- `wiki/` 维护长期知识母页
- `content/posts/` 维护对外收敛后的成品文章
- 高价值文章应回流为 `wiki/topics/` 或 `wiki/concepts/` 的来源

## Current examples

- `content/posts/cs/arch/arch.md`
- `content/posts/cs/dist/circuit-breaker.md`
- `content/posts/cs/golang/go-memory-model.md`
- `content/posts/cs/db/bolt.md`
